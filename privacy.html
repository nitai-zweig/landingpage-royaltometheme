Privacy Policy

The short version

Reign & Gain is a pre-alpha game. This website exists to let people join a waitlist. We collect your email and a consent timestamp, that's it. No cookies, no analytics, no third-party trackers.

What we collect

  • The email address you enter in the waitlist form.
  • A record that you ticked the consent checkbox, and the timestamp when you did.

We do not collect your name, IP address, location, device information, or browsing behavior. We do not use cookies. We do not embed analytics scripts, pixels, or any third-party tracking on this site.

Why we collect it

To send you occasional product updates about Reign & Gain: playtest invites, launch announcements, and major milestones. Nothing else.

Who we share it with

Nobody. We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your email with any third party. Your email is stored in our own database and used only by us to email you about Reign & Gain.

Cookies & tracking

This site uses no cookies and no analytics. There are no third-party scripts embedded. Nothing about your visit is tracked or shared.

How to delete your data

Email us at privacy@reignandgain.example from the address you signed up with and ask us to remove you. We'll delete your record and confirm.

How long we keep it

We keep your email until you ask us to delete it, or until the game ships or the project shuts down, whichever comes first. At that point the waitlist is deleted.

Changes

If we ever change what we collect or how we use it, we'll update this page and email waitlist members before the change takes effect.
